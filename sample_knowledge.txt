# E-Filing System Domain Knowledge

## User Authentication

The e-filing system requires users to authenticate before accessing any features. Authentication is handled through a secure login process that includes:

1. Username/password authentication
2. Two-factor authentication for sensitive operations
3. Session timeout after 30 minutes of inactivity
4. Account lockout after 5 failed login attempts

## Form 2290 Filing Process

Form 2290 is used for Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax. The filing process includes:

1. Pre-filing: Users can prepare their filing before the tax period begins
2. Vehicle information entry: VIN, weight category, and first-used month
3. Tax calculation: Based on vehicle weight and months of use
4. Payment processing: Credit card, direct debit, or electronic funds withdrawal
5. Filing submission: Electronic submission to the IRS
6. Receipt and confirmation: Users receive a stamped Schedule 1 as proof of payment

## Pre-filing Specific Rules

Pre-filing allows users to prepare their Form 2290 filing before the tax period begins. Key rules include:

1. Pre-filing is available 30 days before the start of the tax period (July 1st)
2. Users can edit pre-filed returns any time before submission
3. Pre-filed returns are not submitted to the IRS until the user explicitly submits them
4. Email notifications are sent to remind users to review and submit their pre-filed returns
5. Pre-filed returns automatically include all vehicles from the previous year's filing

## Email Notification System

The system sends various email notifications to users:

1. Confirmation emails after filing submission
2. Reminder emails for upcoming deadlines
3. Notification emails when a pre-filing is edited
4. Marketing emails for new features or services
5. Account security notifications

## Payment Processing

The system supports multiple payment methods:

1. Credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
2. Electronic funds withdrawal (direct debit)
3. IRS payment vouchers for mail-in payments

Payment processing includes:

1. Secure transmission of payment information
2. Real-time validation of payment details
3. Receipt generation after successful payment
4. Refund processing for overpayments or duplicate payments
