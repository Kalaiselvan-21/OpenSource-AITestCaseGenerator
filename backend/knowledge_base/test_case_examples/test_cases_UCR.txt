
### **UCR Self-Filing Registration Test Cases**

---

#### 1. Verify navigation from ‘Start Registration’ CTA to USDOT entry page

**Test Steps**

1. Go to the homepage.
2. Click the ‘Start Registration’ CTA.

**Expected Result**

* User is navigated to the USDOT entry page.
* URL matches the design reference.

---

#### 2. Validate empty USDOT field submission

**Test Steps**

1. On the USDOT entry page, leave the USDOT field empty.
2. Click ‘Continue’.

**Expected Result**

* Appropriate field validation error message is displayed as per TRUC-360.
* ‘Continue’ CTA remains disabled until valid input is provided.

---

#### 3. Validate invalid USDOT number format

**Test Steps**

1. Enter an invalid USDOT number format (e.g., alphabets or less than required digits).
2. Click ‘Continue’.

**Expected Result**

* Validation error displayed according to TRUC-360.

---

#### 4. Validate non-existing USDOT number

**Test Steps**

1. Enter a USDOT number not found in the database.
2. Click ‘Continue’.

**Expected Result**

* Header message: "We could not find the details for this USDOT number."
* CTA displayed to re-enter USDOT number.
* Clicking CTA navigates to the USDOT entry page.

---

#### 5. Validate existing USDOT number found in database

**Test Steps**

1. Enter a valid USDOT number found in the database.
2. Click ‘Continue’.

**Expected Result**

* Header message: "We successfully found the details for this USDOT number."
* Hashed details displayed as per design.
* Email & Mobile Phone fields displayed as mandatory.

---

#### 6. Validate mandatory field enforcement (Email & Mobile)

**Test Steps**

1. Leave Email or Mobile Phone empty.
2. Click ‘Start Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Mandatory field validation message displayed.
* Form submission blocked.

---

#### 7. Verify USDOT number change functionality

**Test Steps**

1. On the USDOT found page, click ‘Change USDOT’.
2. Edit the USDOT number.

**Expected Result**

* All other fields and CTAs are disabled except ‘Update’ and ‘Cancel’.
* Clicking ‘Update’ re-searches database with new USDOT.
* Clicking ‘Cancel’ restores original USDOT and re-enables form fields.

---

#### 8. Navigate to UCR self-filing form after valid details

**Test Steps**

1. Enter valid details on USDOT found page.
2. Click ‘Start Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Lands on Form Page 1 with pre-filled data where available.

---

#### 9. Validate Form Page 1 default registration year

**Test Steps**

1. Load Form Page 1 before September 30, 2025.
2. Check dropdown values.

**Expected Result**

* Default year: 2025.
* Dropdown contains 2024 and 2025.

---

#### 10. Validate Form Page 1 year selection after October 1, 2025

**Test Steps**

1. Load Form Page 1 after October 1, 2025.
2. Check dropdown values.

**Expected Result**

* Default year: 2026.
* Dropdown contains 2025 and 2026.

---

#### 11. Verify classification selection

**Test Steps**

1. Select each classification option individually and in combination.

**Expected Result**

* Multiple selections allowed.
* If only Broker/Leasing selected and vehicle count > 0 → special prompt displayed and skip to Form Page 3.

---

#### 12. Verify Continue Registration CTA enablement on Form Page 1

**Test Steps**

1. Try clicking ‘Continue Registration’ without filling all mandatory fields.

**Expected Result**

* CTA remains disabled until all mandatory fields are filled.

---

#### 13. Validate navigation from Form Page 1 to Form Page 2

**Test Steps**

1. Fill all mandatory fields.
2. Click ‘Continue Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Lands on Form Page 2.
* Progress bar shows 50%.

---

#### 14. Validate Add/Exclude vehicle logic on Form Page 2

**Test Steps**

1. Select ‘Yes’ for Add/Exclude option.
2. Fill additional and exclusion counts.

**Expected Result**

* Total Vehicles computed correctly = Pre-fetched + Additional - Excluded.

---

#### 15. Validate Go Back functionality on Form Page 2

**Test Steps**

1. Click ‘Go Back’.

**Expected Result**

* Returns to Form Page 1 with previously entered data intact.

---

#### 16. Validate Form Page 3 pre-filled billing info

**Test Steps**

1. Load Form Page 3.

**Expected Result**

* Billing info pre-filled from MCS Principal Address by default.
* Option to pre-fill from Mailing Address available.

---

#### 17. Validate ACH/E-Check default selection and mandatory fields

**Test Steps**

1. Keep ACH/E-Check selected.
2. Leave Account Number/Confirm/Routing Number empty.
3. Try to continue.

**Expected Result**

* Validation messages displayed for mandatory fields.

---

#### 18. Validate Mail-In Payment option

**Test Steps**

1. Select Mail-In Payment.
2. Continue.

**Expected Result**

* No ACH/E-Check field validations triggered.
* Lands on Pay Service Fee page.

---

#### 19. Validate Pay Service Fee page

**Test Steps**

1. Review service fee details.
2. Click ‘Pay & Finish’.

**Expected Result**

* Lands on Confirmation Page upon successful transaction.

---

#### 20. Verify Confirmation Page scenarios

**Test Steps**

1. Complete filing with different triggers (Biennial Due, USDOT inactive, Vehicle count updated).

**Expected Result**

* Corresponding message shown based on trigger.
* Discount coupon displayed if applicable.

---

#### 21. Validate email and SMS notifications on Mark Submitted

**Test Steps**

1. CS agent marks self-submitted filing as Submitted.

**Expected Result**

* Email sent as per UCR Tracker config.
* SMS with correct template sent.

#### 22. Validate email and SMS notifications on Mark Complete

### **Negative Test Cases for UCR Self-Filing Flow**

---

#### 1. Submit USDOT field with special characters

**Test Steps**

1. On USDOT entry page, enter a value like `1234@#$`.
2. Click ‘Continue’.

**Expected Result**

* Error message: “Invalid USDOT number format” displayed as per TRUC-360.
* User cannot proceed.

---

#### 2. Submit USDOT field with fewer than required digits

**Test Steps**

1. Enter a USDOT number shorter than required length.
2. Click ‘Continue’.

**Expected Result**

* Error message displayed according to field validation rules.

---

#### 3. Submit USDOT field with leading/trailing spaces

**Test Steps**

1. Enter a valid USDOT number but add spaces at start/end.
2. Click ‘Continue’.

**Expected Result**

* Spaces are trimmed OR validation error shown based on TRUC-360.

---

#### 4. Leave all mandatory fields blank on USDOT found page

**Test Steps**

1. Enter valid USDOT number.
2. On USDOT found page, leave Email and Mobile Phone blank.
3. Click ‘Start Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Error messages displayed for both fields.
* CTA remains disabled until valid data entered.

---

#### 5. Enter invalid email format

**Test Steps**

1. On USDOT found page, enter “abc\@com” as Email.
2. Fill other fields validly.
3. Click ‘Start Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Validation error: “Enter a valid email address” displayed.

---

#### 6. Enter invalid phone number format

**Test Steps**

1. Enter a phone number with letters or fewer digits (e.g., `12345abc`).
2. Click ‘Start Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Validation error for invalid phone format displayed.

---

#### 7. Change USDOT to invalid number

**Test Steps**

1. On USDOT found page, click ‘Change USDOT’.
2. Enter invalid number and click ‘Update’.

**Expected Result**

* Error message: “Invalid USDOT number” displayed.
* Form remains disabled.

---

#### 8. Try to skip classification selection on Form Page 1

**Test Steps**

1. Leave classification unselected.
2. Fill other mandatory fields.
3. Click ‘Continue Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Error message prompting to select at least one classification.

---

#### 9. Enter negative vehicle count in Add/Exclude field

**Test Steps**

1. On Form Page 2, select ‘Yes’ for Add/Exclude.
2. Enter negative numbers in Additional CMVs or Excluded CMVs.
3. Click ‘Continue Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Validation error: “Value cannot be negative” displayed.

---

#### 10. Enter non-numeric vehicle count

**Test Steps**

1. On Form Page 2, enter alphabets in Additional CMVs.
2. Click ‘Continue Registration’.

**Expected Result**

* Validation error for non-numeric input displayed.

---

#### 11. Enter invalid postal code

**Test Steps**

1. On Form Page 3, enter a postal code with letters (if numeric expected) or incorrect length.
2. Click ‘Continue Filing’.

**Expected Result**

* Postal code validation error displayed.

---

#### 12. Leave ACH/E-Check fields blank

**Test Steps**

1. On Form Page 3, keep ACH/E-Check selected.
2. Leave Account Number, Confirm Account Number, and Routing Number blank.
3. Click ‘Continue Filing’.

**Expected Result**

* Mandatory field error messages displayed for each empty field.

---

#### 13. Mismatched Account Number and Confirm Account Number

**Test Steps**

1. On Form Page 3, enter different values in Account Number and Confirm Account Number.
2. Click ‘Continue Filing’.

**Expected Result**

* Error message: “Account number does not match” displayed.

---

#### 14. Enter invalid routing number format

**Test Steps**

1. Enter letters or wrong length routing number.
2. Click ‘Continue Filing’.

**Expected Result**

* Routing number validation error displayed.

---

#### 15. Try to proceed without selecting payment mode

**Test Steps**

1. On Form Page 3, deselect all payment modes (if possible via dev tools).
2. Click ‘Continue Filing’.

**Expected Result**

* Error message: “Please select a payment mode” displayed.

---

#### 16. Attempt to refresh browser during payment

**Test Steps**

1. On Pay Service Fee page, before completing payment, refresh the page.

**Expected Result**

* User warned of potential data loss or redirected to payment start page.

---

#### 17. Attempt to navigate back after payment success

**Test Steps**

1. Complete payment successfully.
2. Click browser Back button.

**Expected Result**

* User should not be able to re-initiate payment accidentally.
* Redirect to Confirmation Page or dashboard.

---
